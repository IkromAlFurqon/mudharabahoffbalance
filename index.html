What is Mudharabah Off Balance Sheet?

Mudharabah Muqayyadah off-balance-sheet is the distribution of Mudharabah funds from capital owners to the business executor through a bank with specific conditions. This type of financing is typically used for shorter-term projects or investments where the investor wants to provide capital without establishing a formal partnership.





Product of Mudharabah Off Balance Sheet

  1. Microfinance: Microfinance refers to small and medium-sized business loans given to entrepreneurs or company owners who need money to establish or develop their enterprises. These loans are frequently arranged as microfinance loans, with smaller loan amounts and shorter repayment terms than standard business loans.
  2. Investment financing
